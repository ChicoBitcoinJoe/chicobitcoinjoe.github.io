MeDao (alpha)

Website Github Whitepaper

The MeDao platform enables any person to find a free market value for their own time as well as allow anyone in the world to gain exposure to the value of that person's time. Every MeDao comes with a MiniMe Token which keeps track of how much time is owed to each holder as well as the capabality to create a snapshot at any point in the tokens history. This comes with many benefits such as easy upgrading of MeDao features, it creates new ways of delivering value to shareholders, and allows for a more graceful recovery in the event of a critical hack or bug.

- learn more -

Once a week the MeDao Founder can receive up to 40 hours worth of tokens. Intrinsically, a single token is worth 1 second of time. Meaning, 1 hours worth of the Founder's tokens can be redeemed for 1 hour of a service they offer. Time does not have to be redeemed immediately and can be saved for later use. This is particularly useful if one believes the Founder's time might be more valuable in the future. An optional burn function is provided for people to make payments to the MeDao thus showing proof of work done by the MeDao Founder.

A simple use case for a MeDao could look as follows: A music artist sells 40 hours of time each week to their fans. This gives the artist a basic income they can rely on while creating music. The artist promises the following to those who hold his time: I will create a snapshot of the token distribution every time I release a song. Profit generated from selling copies of the song go directly to a contract where, at any point, a token holder can exchange their snapshot tokens for an equal distribution of profits held by the contract. Assuming there is demand for the artists song, the token will be traded on an exchange where the value of the token is equal to the current profits held by the contract plus a premium equal to the potential future profits of that song. Better songs will earn more profit and thus have a higher exchange rate. The artists basic income will also increase as the demand for his time increases.

It is possible through "value added" that the artist's time becomes too expensive and no one will want to claim any literal time. This is natural and means the artist's time is better spent making music instead of something like teaching music lessons. Alternatively, the artist can choose to give a discount on their literal time by accepting a fraction of a token hour in exchange for a real hour.

In the future, MeDao can evolve to use things like Bancor (liquidity as a service) and TokenCard (ERC20 token debit card) to create a personal debit/credit card. Shareholders could set a maximum card limit and minimum monthly payment. If the MeDao Founder fails to show proof of work that meets the payment then the card limit would decrease until payments are caught up. Additionally, if proof of work get's too low their exchange rate would suffer and thus their hourly wage as well.

Community (alpha)

Website Github Whitepaper

Community is a decentralized social media platform similar to Reddit but with a few unique features you can't find in traditional social media websites. The current paradigm of centralized networks, top down moderation, and mass data collection, inevitably lead to censorship and questionable business practices. The arrival of decentralized consensus systems provides a road to a world with unparalleled transparency and features while still providing the quality content and seamless user experience expected of a centralized service.

- learn more -

Non-traditional features included in Community are:

  • AutoModeration - The next generation of algorithm based moderation with complete transparency and economic incentives
  • Ad Free - Nothing on the screen but what you choose.
  • Private - Your personal information is never shared or sold to third parties
  • Sybil/Brigade Resistance - The people you interact with have a legitamate stake in what they are saying.
  • Decentralized - Never be censored by a rogue moderator or autofilter again
  • and much more planned for the future!

MeDao Initial Token Offering

You can deposit ether up until the deposit period ends and also withdraw any deposited ether up until the withdraw period ends. An optional commit function is provided to lock in your ether so they are no longer able to be withdrawn. This lets participants in the token offering calculate a minimum ether price per token before the offering ends. After the withdraw period ends all tokens become committed so make sure to withdraw before that time if you want to keep your ether! Tokens are distributed proportionally based on ether deposited. If you deposited 1% of the total ether than you will receive 1% of the token distribution.
